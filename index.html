About Me

I'm an audio engineer and music producer with over a decade of hands-on experience recording, mixing, editing, and mastering music. I specialize in remote mixing and editing for independent artists, crafting immersive, emotionally-driven mixes that reflect and enhance your musical vision.

I started at 14 years old making goofy songs with my buddies in high school. That lead to working in a local DIY studio named Avenue One where we recorded local bands, singer-songwriters, and rappers.

Since then, I’ve had the opportunity to compose for Tanya Nuñez’s 2017 short film Dios Nunca Muere (God Never Dies), and to produce, mix, or master projects such as Lost Republic’s Empire of Indifference, Kenopsia’s All Things Must Decay, and Aztral Folk’s Eclipse Kaboom.

I am proficient in Reaper and have a strong understanding of signal flow, and advanced mixing techniques, editing (drums, guitars, vocals), and vocal tuning. I’m always learning, evolving, and pushing creative boundaries to deliver results that artists can be proud of.

Whether you're looking to elevate a song, polish a podcast, or bring your project to the finish line, I’m here to make you sound your best.
